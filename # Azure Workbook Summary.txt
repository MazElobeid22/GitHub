# Azure Workbook Summary

## Day 1: Cloud Services and Concepts
- Benefits: scalability, cost-efficiency, collaboration, disaster recovery
- Cloud Models: public, private, hybrid, community (e.g., Netflix, FC Barcelona)
- Service Models:
  - IaaS: infrastructure for big data
  - PaaS: development platforms
  - SaaS: software delivery (e.g., Google Workspace)
- Providers: AWS, Azure, GCP

## Day 2: Ethical and Legal Obligations
- **Computer Misuse Act 1990**: protects against unauthorized access
- **Police and Justice Act 2006**: criminalizes hacking tools
- **Data Security**: consent for sensitive data
- **Copyright & Plagiarism**: legal consequences of violations
